Camps We Offer

Little Cubs Camp

Description: A great way for your child to enjoy some summer fun with other preschoolers! Weekly themes will surround story time and arts & crafts. Week 1 Ocean Exploration, Week 2 A Bug's Life, Week 3 At the Farm, Week 4 Backyard Garden, Week 5 Maine Authors, Week 6 Out in Space, Week 7 Days of the Dinosaurs. Campers will have days filled with outdoor play on the Cape Care playground!

Led By: Preschool Staff
Dates: June 22 - August 7
Location: Community Center / Cape Care Playground
Ages: 3-5 yrs old (Campers must be 3 as of June 15 and must be fully potty/toilet trained)
Hours: 8:30 am to 3:00 pm

Adventure Camp

Description: Adventure Camp offers structured activities for a day packed with fun. Campers will be with children their own age counselor led rotations of mostly outdoor activities including: tennis, archery, basketball, art, field sports, and yard games. Campers will participate in a number of these sometimes wet, sometimes messy, but always fun activities. Weekly theme days add to the fun!

Led By: CECS Staff
Dates: Middle School / Pond Cove Campus
Location: June 22 - August 7
Ages: 1st - 5th Graders
Hours: 8:30 am to 3:00 pm (see updated drop-off times)

Teen Scene

Description: This camp offers tweens and teens the chance to hang out with friends while participating in some great activities suited just for them. Teen Scene incorporates daily on-campus sports activities, arts and crafts, and games!

Led By: CECS Staff
Dates: Middle School Campus
Location: June 22 - August 7
Ages: 6th - 9th Graders
Hours: 8:30 am to 3:00 pm
